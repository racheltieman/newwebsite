Welcome to Sip The Tea with RT!

I love drama, just not in my own life. So here we are, gossiping, dishing, and serving all the tea on everyone else's.
I’m from a boring, conservative town in New Jersey. Yes, bagels, diners, and stereotypical high school football was my life. However, me being the glamorous and trendy self, didn’t like that life. I connected much more with Keeping Up With The Kardashians than Gilmore Girls. Surprisingly, going to Central New York pushed me to flourish.
Syracuse University has allowed me to spread my wings and develop into the most extra version of myself. The version that is here today to serve up all the realness and opinions, that no one probably asked for!
My dad got lucky, or unlucky, depends on who you ask. He has three daughters (I think that's pretty self explanatory). We love shopping, makeup, luxury products (again self explanatory). However, my little sister and I blessed our father by playing hockey for four years of our life. It helped with my aggression (see! It evens out).
You can’t choose your family, but damn, did I get lucky. My sisters, my mom and I are the sickest girl clique, and sorry, it’s exclusive.
You now should have a better picture of who I am (make sure to add glitter). Escape, read, and have fun as we talk about all the important not-so-important news of what's going on in other people’s lives!
