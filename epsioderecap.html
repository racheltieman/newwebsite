Episode Recaps!

My favorite episode of the year is back and the magic is long overdue.

The Dancing With the Stars Disney episode is here and “oh boy”, it was magical. Here is a recap of some of the notable dances of the night.

Carole Baskin and Pasha Pashkov: Shocker! Carole Baskin dances to The Lion King and I wish I could unsee it.
Why is she still here? I sincerely apologize to Simba. This was offensive.
Bruno Tonioli agreed. He said ″I am stunned. I actually have never ever see a samba like this one.
You just walked through it. Samba needs life, bounce, and energy. She just can't sleepwalk through a Samba.″
Rawr!
Score: 12 out of 30

Skai Jackson and Alan Bersten: I have no dancing expertise but I really do think Skai is a solid competitor.
Last week she really messed up and was looking for a redemption. I thought it was a solid dance
to The Princess and The Frog “Almost There”. But the judges weren’t thrilled. She has potential and so their
tough love is to push her.
Score: 18 out of 30

Justina Machado and Sasha Farber: Justina kills it every week. Her energy, spunk, and sass comes out into every dance.
Justina performed a Charleston to ″Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious”. The judges critiqued her on some technicalities
which brought down her score. However from the normal viewer, I thought it was supercalifragilisticexpialidocious.
Score: 19 out of 30

Kaitlyn Bristowe and Artem Chigvintsev: Former Bachelorette seems to be following Hannah B’s lead as the front runner of the season.
Kaitlyn performed the foxtrot to Moana’s “How Far I’ll Go”. Kaitlyn had poise and flowed stunningly.
The judges called it the best dance of the night.
Score: 23 out of 30

Nev Schulman and Jenna Johnson: My favorite dance of the night is host of Catfish, Nev.
He danced Argentine tango to ″Angelica,″ from Pirates of the Caribbean.
His characterization was spot-on and he embodied his character perfectly. Ahoy!
Score: 24 out of 30 (the highest of the season so far)
