News!

The winners of Love Island USA were crowned! The final couples were Justine and Caleb, Cely and Johnny, Laurel and Carrington and Moira and Calvin.
On their last day they all enjoyed romantic brunches by the pool before they all gussied up for the big night.
America has voted the winners of 100,000 were… Justine and Caleb! Justine and Caleb are the franchise’s first black winners.
